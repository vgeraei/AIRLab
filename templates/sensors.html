About Us

This project consist of a network of integrated sensors which gather data from the environment. This data enables us provide the user with all sorts of usefull application such as reducing energy consumption, statistical data and adapting to human behaviour.

Our Goals

Energy Consumption

We aim to reduce the energy consumption of the room in any way possible. More than 60% of energy consumption comes from the lighting, heating and cooling which can be drastically reduced in smart environment.

Data Collection

The Data collected by this project can either be used for the reaserch purposes or economical reasons.

Machine Learning

Machine Learning is the heart and the future of smart environments.

Our Services

Through this service you can monitor the condition of your room and be aware of the statistics at any time.

Conveniece

Our main goal is to ensure that the user is in the most desirable condition possible. We can achieve this by adapt to users' habit and set the environment the users' preferred prefrences.

Monitoring

You can be aware of every detailed information that is happening around you. Distance is not a problem and you can also access the data in the past since our system is equipped with a powerful database.

Controller

Our Android app enables you to control your room through internet at any distance and anytime. You can also monitor your room via any device equipped with web service.
